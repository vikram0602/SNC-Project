Information for International Customers

Shipping + Customs

International orders ship via UPS Express service. Generally, shipments are delivered within 4 business days of shipment. UPS will ship your computer to the nearest customs office. Once customs clears the package, you will be contacted for delivery authorization to your residence or place of business.

UPS Express service includes shipment and insurance. System76, Inc. does not collect taxes or duties for orders being shipped internationally. To determine taxes or duties owed, if any, please contact your customs office with both the description of your order and its total price.

All System76 prices are in US Dollar (USD).

Keyboards + Adapters

System76 manufactures US keyboard layouts on all laptops and United Kingdom keyboard layouts on select laptops. Please check the laptop configuration page to determine if the United Kingdom keyboard is available. US layouts are recommended for customers in the US, Canada, Mexico, Australia and New Zealand. The International UK keyboard includes additional keys and is recommended for all other countries. Keyboard stickers may be required for your specific language.

System76 products are compatible with world wide power grids. You may need to purchase a plug adapter for your computer.

General Shipping Information

The Process
