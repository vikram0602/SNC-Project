Who is System76?

A) a diverse team of creators, makers, and builders

B) a small company innovating the next big things

C) a group of extremely hard-core nerds

D) all of the above

Carl

Director of Absolutely Everything

Carl is a star gazing dreamer who’s still saving for a flight into space and assumes anything is possible even when it’s ridiculously difficult.

He started this crazy train so it’s really his fault we’re constantly doing all of this mind-blowing ****.

Jason

Captain of Engineering

Jason likes thinking about things and building stuff. He makes sure all System76 hardware plays well with Linux out of the box and that our customers always recieve an insanely up-to-date Ubuntu install.

In the face of ambiguity, he refuses the temptation to guess.

Kate

Brand Alchemist

Kate took System76’s base logo and transmuted it into a luminous brand. Hired for her ability to discuss either Star Wars or Star Trek plus her intense love of old school Nintendo, Marvel comics, and robots, she makes sure our brand voice stays firmly off the cuff. She wrote parts of everyone’s bios and all of Ian’s.

Cassidy

UI/UX Programming Sorceror

Cassidy’s a hybrid open source + UX dork who takes Kate’s awesome designs and brings them to life. A normal day in the office will include fiddling with a new tech gadget, geeking out over Star Wars, imbibing copious amounts of caffeine, or riding his red Razor scooter. Sometimes all at once.

Ben

Infra-Structural Engineer

Ben keeps the website running perfectly across all platforms and devices. He relocated to Colorado to be closer to snow.

And family.

But mostly snow.

Louisa

Marketing Strategist

Louisa is an inquisitive marketer fueled by junk food and humor. Laser focused when troubleshooting and hypothesizing, she loves playing devil’s advocate (even to her own theories). Her comrades know the punchline when asked, “Do you like fish sticks?” and instinctively sing the final lyric when hearing, “Catherine Zeta Jones – she dips beneath lasers _________.”

Björn

Manager of Absolutely Everything

Björn has found the complete answer to the ultimate question of life, the universe, and everything: it’s skiing, on a 42 degree slope. He obsessively checks weather reports throughout ski season. Summer makes him sad.

On weekdays, he keeps everything in the office running flawlessly.

Ian

Vice-Consul of Customer Relations

Ian was on the phone assisting customers for the entirety of the 48 hours he was given to write a bio. He’s very dedicated. He loves his job even more than the breakfast burritos from the food truck around the corner which is objectively and undeniably better than the one on the opposite corner in every way, don’t even lie.

Sam

Commercial Account Manager

Sam works hard and plays harder. If he’s not in the office selling brilliant computers to System76’s awesome commercial customers, he’s probably skiing. His passion for the sport is rivaled only by that of Björn and perhaps the country of Slovenia (to which we now ship, thanks to him!).

Emma

Consumer Account Manager

Emma is a friendly, passionate Ubuntu fanatic motivated first and foremost by helping our consumers find their perfect System76 machine. She has a long-standing addiction devotion to the color pink and her favorite things include kittens, pizza, and classic arcade games.

Mark

Tech Support Fireman

Mark studiously examines support tickets, the intricacies of Linux, and peppers on the Scoville scale.

Wherever the fires of confusion rage … he’ll be there. To put them out. Metaphorically?

You get it.

Tony

“That Hardware Sure is Quality” Technician

Never afraid to tear things down and rebuild from scratch, Tony loves to test limits; he’s primarily fueled by deep-fried Hot Pockets and Mtn Dew. He hails from Brooklyn but you’ll only hear it when he’s trying to infect the accent-prone in the office, namely Carl and Kate.

Todd

Master of Agility

Todd joined System76 to make us run more efficiently with his agile-fu. When he’s not working to keep us all busy, he’s a space geek; he has a real observatory. In his backyard. He takes pictures of the universe. NBD.

Oh, and he has a full-size Tardis too.

Matt

Accounting for Everything

Part-time bookkeeper, full-time ragamuffin, Matt has been counting beans and taking names for over a decade in the Mile High City. Already a Linux (Fedora) user, he fit right in with the System76 mission to bring superior open source software to the masses, which is a fancy way of saying he’s also a nerd.

C) a group of extremely hard-core nerds

D) all of the above
